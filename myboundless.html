    FAQ

  • What Is My Boundless?

    • My Boundless is our unique program to reward customers for dining with us. We offer a variety of prizes that can be redeemed with points earned from purchases.


  • How does it work?

    • Evertime you make a purchase, our employees will ask you for your My Boundless card. With it we will look up your account information and add points corresponding to your purchase. From your mobile device or computer, you can redeem rewards with these points.


  • How many points do I get for...?

    • The point system is quite simple actually. Everyone has different preferences on food and different food items cost different prices. So, rather than count the dollars you spend, we count the amount of servings you order. Each serving translates to 100 points. You can also get anywhere between 10-100 points for answering surveys on the website about your taste buds.


  • How many points do I have?

    • To check how many points you have, simply login above to see your point balance. You can redeem your points for rewards or stack them up for larger rewards.


  • How do I redeem offers?

    • To redeem offers go to Offers page once logged in and press on the offer you wish to redeem. You will then be mailed a code to give to the cashiers at Boundless in order to have your offer completed.


  • Where can I get started?!

    • Come visit us and talk to any of our employees to get started or just make an account by clicking here.

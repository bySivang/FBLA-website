HOW BOUNDLESS CAME TO BE

When the founder of Boundless, Christopher Howard, was growing up he was told stories about a place near his father's hometown. His father told Christopher numerous stories on what the place consisted of, how it always had the best games, and why he even decided to have his 18th Birthday Party there to commerate an ending to a childhood. When Christopher's Dad fell ill of cerioiuis diease, Christopher knew what he had to do to make him feel better. With some saved up money he decided to open up this vision his father had described. Because his father had always decribed the expierence as unlimited, Christipher knew exactly what to call it "Boundless".

WHAT BOUNDLESS IS ABOUT

Christopher wanted Boundless to resonate his father's stories, thus he made the place for kids and adults and made it without bounderies or limits of any kind. He wanted kids to expierence true happiness.

OUR GRAND PROMISE

When Christopher finally showed his father Boundless, his father could not be happier. His father told Christopher something that later became our grand promise. At Boundless we make memories, and not just casual parties. We want to make not just your expierence immeasurable, but also live up to Christopher's expectations!
